Surf 'N Turf

Description

Savory grilled shrimp served with a perfectly juicy cut of filet mignon. Perfect for a romantic date night or a fancy occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tbsp minced onion
  • 1 tbsp white wine
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 12 medium shrimp, peeled/deveined
  • 2 (4oz) filet mignon steaks
  • 1 tsp steak seasoning

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, butter, onion, wine, lemon juice, seasoning, garlic, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add shrimp to the mix. Coat thorougly and let marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat pan/grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil. Coat steaks with olive oil and seasoning.
  4. Cook steaks 7-8 minutes on each side until outside is crisp and inside is slightly pink. Transfer to a plate.
  5. Grill shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side.
  6. Serve shrimp and steak, adding garnish to taste. Enjoy!
