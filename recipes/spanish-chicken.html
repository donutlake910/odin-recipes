Spanish Chicken and Rice

Description

An easy, quick, delicious dish with low clean up time. Perfect for those lazy nights!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound boneless chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 2 cups of water
  • 16 oz of yellow rice
  • 1 cup of frozen veggies

Steps

  1. Heat a large pan to medium-high heat. Add olive oil, garlic and onion. Cook until fragrant and slightly transparent.
  2. Add chicken and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add water and bring to a boil. Add yellow rice, covered with a lid, and let simmer over medium heat for about 20 minutes.
  4. Add veggies and cook until thawed. Stir thorougly and serve.
