Apple Pie

Description

Deliciously sweet, homemade apple pie. Best served with a side of ice cream!

Ingredients

  • 8 apples
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 double-crust pie pastry, thawed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees and peel/core apples, thinly sliced.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and stir into paste.
  3. Add both sugars and water and bring to a boil for 3-5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  4. Press one pastry into the bottom of a 9-inch pan. Cut a strip of pastry into eight 1-inch strips.
  5. Place apple slices around the pan. Lay four pastry strips over the top, evenly spaced.
  6. Use remaining strips to make a lattice crust. Brush the sugar-butter mixture over the top of the crust and pour the remainder to cover the apples.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes. Reduce oven to 350 degrees and cook another 40 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes, serve and enjoy!
